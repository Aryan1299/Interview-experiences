Marketing – Fresher – B.Tech

I had my SP Jain interview today… GI 1: 2 panelists: both male, one from finance (20 years of experience in RBI) the other was from quants department (don’t remember the exact name) Panel details: I was from marketing, 2 finance, 2 IM

Tell me about yourself interestingly without including what we already know (same question to all) How can you apply electrical and electronics engineering in marketing… If u are so good I marketing, sell any product from your domain to the girl sitting next to you. (Went very much down the hill as the girl beside me was totally hell-bent on not getting persuaded by me and kept saying “no” to every question of mine. Don’t know why)

what kind of company would you like to work in? (I gave very cliche answers here and one of the interviewers said I not being very exciting and not giving any new answers. I corrected myself and said something better). Later the topic drifted towards how to make pizzas better as one guy had mentioned he loves cooking.

What is the problem if we ask all the customers how much customization they want? (Answered by saying they would take up time and reduce productivity.. he seemed quite satisfied)

After this, we waited for round-2. Our panel was the first panel of the day where all of us were selected for round 2. No elimination.

GI 2: Panelists: 1 male 1 female Panel details: 2 marketing 2 finance 1 Operations

The whole interview was about our psychometric tests… They had formed a character image about us and pushed us to the extreme to admit us to do something that’s the opposite of what we wrote.

Example: I had said I hate lies… He asked, “do u think marketing people never lie?”. I said, ” they do but I will choose not to lie”. “But how can you sell without lying” “Using emotional connections”…

A couple of follow up questions…

They tried to break everyone else too… Me and 2 others didn’t break… The last two broke. One had said he avoids confrontations but later admitted to confrontation being a better choice… Then the interviewer admitted the interview was a bit pushy… But that was just to know us better. They wished us the best of lucks and the interview ended. They gave us wearable pen drives.

Personal advice… Don’t budge from what you said no matter how much they push you…
